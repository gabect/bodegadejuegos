Frontline Echo

Dodge the red blocker, move through the maze, and reach the green goal.

Your browser needs HTML5 canvas support to play Frontline Echo.

Controls

WASD move • Mouse look after clicking • ←/→ look • Action button: click or Space • R resets action

Objective

Tag the red blocker three times, then reach the green goal. Keep your distance when it rolls close.
