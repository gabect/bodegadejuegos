Frontline Echo

Avoid the red patrol, move through the trench layout, and reach the green extraction zone.

Your browser needs HTML5 canvas support to play Frontline Echo.

Controls

WASD move • Mouse look after clicking • ←/→ look • Left click/Space shoot • R reload

Objective

Defeat the red patrol with 3 hits, then reach the green extraction zone. The patrol still hurts you up close.
